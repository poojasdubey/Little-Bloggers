This is Little Bloggers, a free, Storyteller blog

We are here to take your young ones to a world of imagination they are not aware about...

Ramayana

Ancient Indian epic stories which narrates the struggle of the divine prince Rama to rescue his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana.

Mahabharata

Epic narrative of the Kurukshetra War and the fates of the Kaurava and the Pandava princes.

Panchtantra

An ancient Indian collection of interrelated animal fables in verse and prose, arranged within a frame story.

Jaatak Tales

A voluminous body of literature native to India concerning the previous births of Gautama Buddha. These are the stories that tell about the previous lives of the Buddha, in both human and animal form.

Foreign Writers

Some epic stories by renowned writers from around the world
